Our Fellowship

At RCCG YAYA, we are dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth and development of the youth within the Redeemed Christian Church of God. We understand that young people are the future of our church and society — and we're committed to equipping them with the faith, knowledge, and values to excel in every aspect of life.

The RCCG Youth and Young Adults (YAYA) Fellowship aims to empower and equip young people for effective ministry, leadership, and spiritual growth, fostering a generation rooted in God's word and prayer.

Vision Statement

  • Raise a Burning Generation: Vibrant, spirit-filled, and purpose-driven youth grounded in the Word of God and prayer.
  • Provide a Platform: Creating a space where young people encounter Jesus and prepare to propagate the Gospel.
  • Establishment: Walking in the fullness of God and impacting the world for Christ.

Mission Statement

  • Evangelism: Winning the youth for the Kingdom.
  • Leadership: Building Christian leaders for every sector.
  • Empowerment: Nurturing spiritual growth and social responsibility.
  • Community: Building capacity for impactful change.

Core Values

Passion for God
Love for People
Excellence
Integrity

Part of RCCG Worldwide

"To make heaven, to take as many people with us, and to have a member of the church in every family of all nations."
